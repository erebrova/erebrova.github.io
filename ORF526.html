General course information:

This is a graduate introduction to probability theory. Topics include: an introduction to mathematical probability theory, law of large numbers, central limit theorem, conditioning, filtrations and stopping times, Markov processes and martingales in discrete and continuous time. Time permitting, there is an introduction to Poisson processes, and Brownian motion.
