Tentative list of topics:

# 1
Introduction and math review
# 2
Optimality conditions for unconstrained optimization
# 3
Convex sets and functions, Caratheodory and Approximate Caratheodory theorems
# 4
Convex optimization problem. Separability of convex sets and Farkas lemma
# 5
Farkas lemma and strong duality of linear programming.
# 6
Min vertex cover = max matching on bipartite graphs. Convex functions and optimzality
# 7
Properties of convex functions continued
# 8
Support vector machines
# 9
Hierarchy of classes of convex problems
# 10
Duality of Semidefinite (SDP) and Conic Programming
# 11
Eigenvalue optimization as SDP
# 12
Stability in optimal control as SDP
# 13
Probabilistic estimates as SDP
# 14
Graph combinatorial optimization and SDP relaxation
# 15
Nonconvex quadratic optimization and SDP relaxation
# 16
Complexity theory, hard polynomial optimization
# 17
Approximate integer optimization, Grothendieck's inequality, MAXCUT and other applications
# 18
Other approaches to NP-hard optimization problems
# 19
Solving convex problems, interior points and barrier functions
# 20
Dimension reduction of large-scale optimization problems
